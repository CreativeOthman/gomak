The Chairman

Garba Mohammed Sarkin Kudu

Early Life and Educational Background

Garba Mohammed Sarkin Kudu was born on 12th August 1960, in Gusau, Zamfara state into a Muslim Family. He hails from Sokoto North, Sokoto State, Nigeria. He started his early years education at Dan Sadau Township Primary School, Zamfara State from 1967 to 1973 where he achieved his Primary school leaving certificate. He attended Government Secondary School Gusau, Zamfara State from 1973 to 1979 where he attained his Secondary School leaving certificate. After concluding his secondary school education, Garba proceeded to State of College of Arts and Science, Sokoto State from 1979 to 1982 where he obtained his IJMB Certificate in 1982. He attended Usmanu Danfodio University Sokoto from 1983 to 1987 obtaining a bachelor’s of Arts degree in History with honors and merits and then proceeded for his National Youth Service Corps from 1987-1988 in Gongola State, and attained his NYSC certificate in 1988.

Professional Career

Garba started his professional career where he served as a corp liaison officer and a personal assistance to the chairman of Song Local Government, Gongola state from 1987 to 1988. He later worked in the office of the Military Governor, Sokoto State as Monitoring Officer II at the Establishment and Monitoring Department in 1989. He then worked as a Monitoring Officer I, in the Ministry of Education in Sokoto State fro m 1989-1992. He began working as a Personnel Officer I in the Cabinet Office, office of the military Governor, Sokoto State between 1992 and 1994. Garba began working at Ministry of Information, Home Affairs, Youth, Sports and Culture, Sokoto State as Assistant Director, Personnel Management in 1994. He worked as Assistant Director, Personnel Management, in the Ministry of Education, Sokoto State from 1994 to 1995. He worked as Director Personnel Management at the Sokoto State House of Assembly from 1995 to 1996, Director Personnel Management Government House from 1996 to 1998, and Director Personnel Management Ministry of Water Resources, Sokoto State, from 1998-1999. In 1999, He began working at Sokoto State Liaison Office, Abuja in charge of Abuja and Kaduna until 2000. He worked at Ministry of Finance, Sokoto as Director Personnel Management from January –April 2000, Senior Administrative Officer, Federal Civil Service Commission, Abuja in April 2000, Senior Administrative, State and Local Government Affairs Office of the Vice President, Abuja from May-June 2000, and Senior Administrative Officer in the Federal Ministry of Sports and Social Development, Abuja from 2000-2008. He worked at the Office of Head of service, Executive office of the governor, Sokoto State in April 2008. Garba served as the Permanent Secretary for several departments in Sokoto State, including Department of Higher Education from 2008 to 2013, Ministry of Commerce, Industry and Tourism from 2013 to 2015, Ministry of Environment from 2015 to 2016, Ministry of Water resources in 2016, Ministry of Agriculture from 2016-2017 and Ministry of Information from 2017 till date. He retired from the state service at the rank of Permanent Secretary on the 11th of August 2018.

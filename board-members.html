Garba Mohammed Sarkin Kudu

Designation: Chief Executive Officer/ Managing Director

Garba Mohammed Sarkin Kudu was born on 12th August 1960, in Gusau, Zamfara state into a Muslim Family. He hails from Sokoto North, Sokoto State, Nigeria. He started his early years education at Dan Sadau Township Primary School, Zamfara State from 1967 to 1973 where he achieved his Primary school leaving certificate. He attended Government Secondary School Gusau, Zamfara State from 1973 to 1979 where he attained his Secondary School leaving certificate. After concluding his secondary school education, Garba proceeded to State of College of Arts and Science, Sokoto State from 1979 to 1982 where he obtained his IJMB Certificate in 1982. He attended Usmanu Danfodio University Sokoto from 1983 to 1987 obtaining a bachelor’s of Arts degree in History with honors and merits and then proceeded for his National Youth Service Corps from 1987-1988 in Gongola State, and attained his NYSC certificate in 1988.

Abubakar, Ramatu (Mrs.)

Designation: Director

Ramatu Abubakar hails from Sokoto North area of Sokoto State. She was born on October 12th 1997 in Sokoto State, Nigeria. She attended Crescent Nursery School, Sokoto from 1999-2001 where she obtained her nursery school certificate before proceeding to Federal staff primary school sokoto from 2001-2003 and to Unity International School, Abuja from 2003-2009 where she obtained her primary school and junior secondary school certificates. She attended her senior secondary school Lead British International School from 2009-2012 where she obtained her Higher Secondary School Certificate. She attended Canadian University, Dubai, and UAE from 2013-2017. Abubakar is a first class BBA Accounting and Finance Graduate from the Canadian University Dubai who is currently working on her chartered accountants professional certificate.
